About Sattav Group

Founded in 1993, the Sattva Group has established itself as one of India's most diversified and trusted real estate developers. Headquartered in Bengaluru, the group has spent over three decades shaping the skylines of 7 major cities across the country. With a remarkable track record of delivering over 69 million sq. ft. of world-class residential and commercial spaces, they have become a symbol of architectural brilliance and unwavering quality.

The group's success is rooted in its commitment to innovation, customer-centricity, and sustainable development. Beyond just building structures, Sattva Group focuses on creating self-sustaining ecosystems that enhance the quality of life for its residents. Their portfolio includes high-end luxury residences, state-of-the-art commercial hubs, and retail spaces that set new industry benchmarks. With 28 upcoming projects in the pipeline, Sattva continues to lead with a vision of "Total Transparency" and "Timely Delivery," ensuring that every landmark they build stands the test of time and offers exceptional value to its stakeholders.
